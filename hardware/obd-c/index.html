    OBD-C Cable¶

    The Comma 3x comes with a short OBD-C cable. If you need another one for setups like SASCM, ASCM, SDGM, OBD harness:

    Any USB-C 3.2 cable will work to meet the OBD2-C Spec. Length needed will vary by car.

    Some example options:

    • https://comma.ai/shop/obd-c-cable
    • https://konik.ai/shop/usb-c-cable
